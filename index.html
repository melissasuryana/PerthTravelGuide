Perth city, located on the banks of Swan River, is considered to be one of the most stunning and isolated cities in the world (City of Perth, 2019). Perth offers metropolitan and urban lifestyle, within walking distance to offices, shops and restaurants.

There are more to Perth than city life. Outdoor enthusiasts can enjoy water activities on the Swan River or trail walking and biking on the nearby natural bushland in Kings Park.

Highlights of Perth

Business

In the center of Perth city, there are hundreds of local businesses, makes it a perfect place for corporate events.

Lifestyle

Shop to your heart's content at wide range of boutiques and speciality shops in Murray Street and Hay Street malls.

Public transport

To travel within the Perth CBD, Transperth offers free bus travel within the Perth city boundary.

Getting here

Perth International Airport is connected directly to many global transit hubs (Perth Region Tourism Organisation, 2019). There are many transport options to travel from the airport to Perth city.

Taxis

A taxi trip will take 20 to 30 minutes from the airport to Perth city and it usually costs around AUD30.00 to AUD40.00 (Perth Region Tourism Organisation, 2019). Swan Taxis and Black & White Cabs aree the main taxi providers in Perth (Perth Region Tourism Organisation, 2019).

Rideshares

You can order rideshare services to Perth airport as there is a dedicated rideshare pickup areas in all of the terminals (Perth Region Tourism Organisation, 2019). Uber and Ola are some of the popular rideshare services in Perth (City of Perth, 2019).

Public transport

Transperth offers regular bus services from Perth Airport to the Perth city (Perth Region Tourism Organisation, 2019).

Things to know

Climate

Perth is considered to have mild winters, bringing downpour of rains from June to August (Perth Region Tourism Organisation, 2019). Long and hot summer usually starts from December and last until February (Perth Region Tourism Organisation, 2019).

Rain is unusual during summer, instead you will experience hours of strong sunshine, so be prepared with sunscreen, sunglasses and hats.

Perth surrounding

Consider to travel beyond the Perth CBD to discover further shop and dine experiences. Some of the must-visit suburbs within 10 minutes of drive from Perth:

  • Northbridge: multi cultural food and art paradise.
  • Subiaco: hip and trendy suburb with lots of unique shops and cafes.
  • Burswood: home of Crown Perth restaurants and casino.
  • West Perth: for shop lovers, visit Watertown Brand Outlet in West Perth.
