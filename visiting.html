There are so many different activites that you can do in Perth city, from exploring the public attractions, shop and dine, or enjoy the great outdoor in Elizabeth Quay and Kings Park. With so many choices, there will be something for everyone to enjoy.

Accomodations

Hotels & Motels

With approximately 122 hotels in Perth city, ranging from luxury hotels to budget friendly motels, there are plenty of options to suit any budget (City of Perth, 2019). Some popular choices are Parmelia Hilton, Mercure Hotel, Esplanade Hotels and Intercontinental Perth.

Resorts

If you prefer accomodations with amenities such as spas, fitness centres, restaurants, and attractions, resorts will be the perfect choice! Some of the resorts around Perth are Crown Metropol Perth, Broadwater Resort Como and Pagoda Resort & Spa.

Apartments

Need a long term accomodation? Browse through apartments for rent in the city or along the river. Property rental websites such as Airbnb or Rent.com.au will be a good source to find the right apartment for your needs.

Some of the apartments available in Perth are Adina Apartment Hotel Perth, Citadines St Georges Terrace Perth and Attika Hotel Northbridge.

Activities

Explore the city
  • Visit Bell Tower, the Perth landmark, in Barrack Square.
  • Visit Elizabeth Quay for stunning views of Swan River.
  • Go shopping at the wide range of stores and brands.
  • Enjoy fine dining or hearty meals at restaurants, bars and cafes.
Outdoor natures
  • Go on a trail walk or bike riding at Kings Park
  • Explore the Perth city by bike or Segway.
  • Ride on river cruise to explore Swan River from Perth and beyond.
  • Enjoy water activities such as rowing kayaks on Swan River.

Transportations

Public transport

The Perth public transport network is run by Transperth. Travel within Perth city by bus is free, as free zone transit is applied to the area within Perth CBD (City of Perth, 2019). If you are first time visitors, try Perth CAT buses, as there are real time travel progress displayed inside the bus (Perth Region Tourism Organisation, 2019).

To travel beyond Perth CBD, catch buses from any bus stops along the road. To catch trains, you will need to go to one of the stations: Perth Station, Perth Underground, or Elizabeth Quay Station.

Rideshare and taxis

Riding taxis or ridesharing can be a great option if you still unfamiliar with the public transport system or need to get around fast. There are designated taxi ranks around the hotels and city central streets, such as 140 William Street and Milligan Street (City of Perth, 2019).

Swan Taxis and Black & White Cabs are the main taxi providers in Perth (Perth Region Tourism Organisation, 2019). For the rideshare option, Uber and Ola seems to be the popular choice in Perth (City of Perth, 2019).

Driving

There are many rental car services across Perth with wide range variety of cars to choose from. To rent cars in Perth, you will need a valid driver's license (Perth Region Tourism Organisation, 2019). For international visitors, it is recommended to get an international license (Perth Region Tourism Organisation, 2019).
